Tagore Library

Central Research Library — University of Lucknow

Introduction

Tagore Library is the Central Research Library of the University of Lucknow. It is here that the academic fraternity — both the preceptor and his pupils — could find intellectual food.

It is respected as one of the oldest, well-organised and richest libraries of India. With the advent of Information Technology, this library is also changing its traditional format.

🌐 Electronic Access

Since 2004, access to scholarly electronic journals and databases has been made available through UGC–Infonet E-Journals Consortium via INFLIBNET, Ahmedabad.

More than 10,000 full-text electronic journals & databases are available via inflibnet.ac.in.
